# Automatic Active RulePack Mutation

Status: Blocked by Contract

## Blocker

Rule Discovery and Reflection may propose candidates, but cannot activate
rules.

## Required Contract Or Gate

Manual approval/versioning contract and active RulePack publish policy.

## Boundary

No research or reflection workflow may mutate active RulePack automatically.

## Unblock Step

Define RulePack versioning, manual approval, publish, rollback, and audit
semantics.
